The Founder

The man himself

Nick founded Tree Town Coffee Co. in the midst of a Pandemic! It was always a dream of his to start his own coffee shop. With the help of his Fiance and his brother, they were able to travel the world searching for the perfect coffee beans to roast. In his free time, Nick loves to go camping, play with his crazy dog Grizzly, and play the guitar!
